Full Brand Exposure

Make your brand stand out from the crowd! Your company gets all the attention, while I can fully focus on perfecting my golf game. Your company logo will be visible on all apparel, my golf bag, umbrella (Elite), website and social media publications (Pro).

Flight Connect

Boost you client relationship and marketing management efforts with the addition of me as your ultimate team member to your morning or afternoon tee time flight. A unique experience where the striking similarities between playing a round of competitive tournament golf under tremendous pressure and dealing with high profile business decisions in daily corporate lives, will be one of the main take-aways.

During practicing and playing a 9 or 18-holes round private session I will introduce your business partners to the wonderful world of playing golf at a tour professional level and share key insightful tips & tricks to improve their golf swing. I will take them on a journey of continuous decision making while maintaining a razor-sharp focus and strengthen business relationships in the process.

Pro Tour Clinic Day

Improve your game while meeting other business executives from around the world. Promote your brand, learn from inside the ropes and get inspired by fellow exclusive Founding Members' businesses.

During the Founding Member Pro Tour Clinic Day you will be invited to join round table sessions with captains of industry, attend workshops with business influencers and connect with other members. This informal gathering is a great way to sharpen your golf and business skills!

Charity Chip-in

Golf is a game of ups and downs. It's almost like life itself. I envisioned sharing my success with people less fortunate. As a Founding Member you allow me to share 5% of my prize money with a charity of your choice.

Pro and Elite members both have the option to choose a charity that they believe should deserve a little bit more attention and affection. Each time I win prize money in any of the tournaments I play in, your selected charity also wins.
